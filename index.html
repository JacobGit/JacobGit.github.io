What is the
Hotelie Tag Challenge?

The Hotelie Tag Challenge is an initiative to raise money for the School of Hotel Administration so it can maintain its status as the best school for management in the hospitality industry. The outcome of completing the challenge is three-fold: you will contribute to the SHA annual fund, the visual display of posted luggage tags, and SHA's online presence. So show your Hotelie pride and participate in the challenge today!


Why Give?

  • Scholarships and Financial Aid
  • World Class Faculty Recruitment and Retention
  • Student Travel
  • State of the Art Facilities
  • Educational Resources and Libraries

What is the
Hotelie Tag Challenge?

The Hotelie Tag Challenge is an initiative to raise money for the School of Hotel Administration so it can maintain its status as the best school for management in the hospitality industry. Show your Hotelie pride and participate in the challenge today!

Why Give?

  • Scholarships and Financial Aid
  • World-Class Faculty Recruitment and Retention
  • Student Travel
  • State-of-the-Art Facilities
  • Educational Resources and Libraries
